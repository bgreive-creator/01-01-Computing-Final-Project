    Mental Health and Sleep

    The treemap allows us to see the differences between men and women in sleep levels and mental health status. In men, it is clear that the more sleep one gets, the better their mental health status. Men who have poor mental health only get 6.4 hours of sleep on average a night. Women do not exhibit as strong of a relationship. Even among those with poor mental health, they are getting above average sleep time. Women with good mental health are an outlier category that is below the average sleep level.
